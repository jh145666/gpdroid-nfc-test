# CodeBook NFC User Guide

CodeBook is a secure offline password manager stored on your Java Card (J3R200). It supports up to 120 records.

---

## 1. Initial Setup
If your card is brand new, you will see the **Initialization** screen.

1.  Enter a new PIN (e.g., 123456) twice.
2.  Tap your NFC card to the back of your phone.
3.  The app will format the card and set your PIN.

---

## 2. Authentication
If the app was reinstalled or the card is already initialized:

1.  Enter your PIN in the **Login** screen.
2.  Tap the card to enter the Main Interface.

---

## 3. Core Operations
In the main interface, select an action from the top bar and enter the required info:

### ➕ Add Record
- **Action:** Select "Add".
- **Input:** Enter a "Key" (e.g., Gmail) and a "Value" (your password).
- **Execution:** Enter your PIN below and tap the card.

### 🔍 Search
- **Action:** Select "Search".
- **Input:** Enter the Key you want to find.
- **Execution:** Enter your PIN and tap the card.

### 📋 List All
- **Action:** Select "List".
- **Execution:** Enter your PIN and tap the card. All records (up to 120) will be displayed.

### 🗑️ Delete
- **Action:** Select "Delete".
- **Input:** Enter the **Record Number** (e.g., 1, 30) from the list.
- **Execution:** Enter your PIN and tap the card.

---

## 4. Maintenance
### 🔐 Reset PIN
- Select "Reset PIN", enter your current PIN, then provide the new PIN twice and tap the card.

### ⚠️ Wipe All
- Select "Wipe" and tap the card to permanently delete ALL data.

---

## 💡 Tips
- **Max Capacity:** 120 records.
- **Data Safety:** Data is stored ONLY on the card. If you lose the card, data is lost.
- **Connection:** Keep the card still against the phone until the operation completes.
